Reality checks & assumptions

▼

Benchmarks from the Vatera study:

  • North Aegean hotel bed-occupancy ~39.6% annual avg (2024 ELSTAT) — this is your reality check on rooms.
  • Booking.com Vatera spot rates: ~€56/night early June; £56–£80+ shoulder. Premium positioning is unlikely.
  • ~96% of international airport arrivals to Lesvos happen May–Sep — winter operation needs local/Greek demand.
  • Greece KYE restaurant licence fee (paravolo): €75–€375; beach concession deadline 28 Feb (special 15 Mar 2026).
  • Go/no-go trigger: in-season ~€1,000–€1,300/day combined beach + taverna sales = the plan works under Normal.

How calculations work:

  • Each month is classed Peak / Shoulder / Low / Off via the calendar grid above. Click a month to cycle.
  • Revenue per stream = days_active × volume × rate, summed across months for that stream's class.
  • Self-managed adds a per-turnover cleaning cost but removes the reception salary line.
  • Owner-operated mode means owner draws no salary — toggle off to add a market wage.
  • Numbers are pre-tax / pre-finance. VAT not modelled (assume net).
